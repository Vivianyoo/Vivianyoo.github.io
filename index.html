About

Research Focus

My current interests lie in the intersection of computer graphics, Unreal Engine, MetaHuman animation, XR systems, and virtual character perception. I am especially interested in how animation, facial expression, motion style, and interactive behaviour influence the way users perceive digital humans and virtual avatars.
